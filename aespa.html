æspais a South Korean girl group formed by SM Entertainment. The group consists of four members: Karina, Giselle, Winter and Ningning. They debuted on November 17, 2020 with the single, "Black Mamba". The group name "aespa" is a combination of "æ," which expresses "Avatar X Experience," and the English word "aspect," which means both sides.
